Urgent Care Wait Time Management System.
Please Check Our Project.

Problem

Although the main goal of urgent care facilities is helping patients who are in immediate need, most of patients end up waiting too long.

Solution

Gigsynergy is providing patients with an online check in system that they can reserve their spots before stepping in the facility.

Project Home Page



Care Finder Page

This page provides patient with a list of nearby urgentcares, their waiting time, addresses and ratings. Patient can get this list simply by entering city name or zipcode.



Check-In page

After choosing desired urgent care facility, patient is redirected to this page which she needs to fill out this form. By clicking on check In button, her spot is reserved!
